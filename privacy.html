Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Applies to groundworkaustralia.com.au and foundations.groundworkaustralia.com.au

Groundwork Australia (ABN 21 522 799 850) is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose your personal information.

2. What personal information we collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

From the contact form

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your business type
  • The content of your enquiry

From Groundwork Foundations (subscribers)

  • Your name and email address (provided during signup)
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely by Stripe — we do not store card details)
  • Subscription plan and status
  • Usage data — which tools you use and documents you generate
  • Content you enter into tools (business names, employee details, situation descriptions)

From website visits

  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Geographic location (country and city level)
  • Referring website or search terms

Website analytics are collected via Google Analytics. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

3. How we collect personal information

We collect personal information directly from you when you:

  • Submit the contact form on our website
  • Create an account on Groundwork Foundations
  • Subscribe to a paid plan
  • Use any tool within the Foundations platform
  • Contact us by email or phone

We also collect information automatically through cookies and analytics tools when you visit our websites.

4. Why we collect and use your information

We collect and use your personal information to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide HR consulting services
  • Create and manage your Groundwork Foundations account
  • Process your subscription and payments
  • Provide access to the tools and features you have subscribed to
  • Improve our platform and services
  • Send service-related communications (account updates, billing notices)
  • Comply with legal obligations

We will only use your personal information for the purpose for which it was collected, or for a directly related purpose that you would reasonably expect.

5. Marketing communications

We may send you information about our services, new tools, or HR updates if you have indicated you wish to receive such communications, or if it is reasonably expected in the context of our relationship. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by emailing hello@groundworkaustralia.com.au or by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

6. Disclosure of your information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may disclose your information to:

Service providers

  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy
  • Supabase — secure database hosting for account and subscription data
  • Netlify — website and platform hosting
  • Google — analytics and email services
  • Anthropic — AI processing for Professional tier tools. Content you enter into AI tools is processed by Anthropic's API

These providers are engaged to help us deliver our services and are required to handle your information in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to comply with legal process, protect our rights, or ensure the safety of our users.

7. AI-powered tools

Groundwork Foundations Professional tier tools use AI technology provided by Anthropic. When you use these tools, the information you enter (such as business details, employee information, or situation descriptions) is sent to Anthropic's API for processing to generate your document or guide.

We recommend you do not enter sensitive personal information about identifiable individuals beyond what is necessary to generate a useful document. Documents generated are for your use only and should be reviewed by a qualified HR professional before use.

8. How we store and protect your information

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, and disclosure. Security measures include:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) across all our websites
  • Secure database hosting with access controls
  • Payment processing via Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure — we never store payment card details
  • User authentication via Supabase with encrypted passwords

While we take reasonable precautions, no data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed as completely secure.

9. How long we keep your information

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active, or as long as necessary to provide services and comply with our legal obligations. If you close your account, we will delete or de-identify your personal information within 90 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

10. Cookies

Our websites use cookies to improve your experience and collect analytics data. Cookies we use include:

  • Google Analytics cookies — to understand how visitors use our site (anonymous, aggregated data)
  • Session cookies — to maintain your login session on the Foundations platform

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of the Foundations platform.

11. Access, correction, and deletion

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@groundworkaustralia.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

12. Complaints

If you believe we have breached your privacy rights or the Australian Privacy Principles, please contact us first so we can try to resolve the issue. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au or by calling 1300 363 992.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our websites or services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
