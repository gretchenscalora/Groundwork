If you can fill in a form and read a result, you can use Groundwork Foundations. The tools are built for business owners and managers, not HR professionals. Plain English throughout.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-in, no cancellation fees. Cancel through your account any time and your subscription ends at the close of your current billing period.

Are the documents legally compliant?

The tools generate draft documents informed by Australian employment law and relevant Modern Awards. They are starting points for professional review — not finished legal documents. We always recommend having Gretchen review any document before you issue it to staff.

What if I'm already in a Fair Work matter?

The IR case prep assistant is built for exactly this situation. It won't replace a lawyer for a complex hearing, but it will help you understand your position, gather the right documents, and know what to say at conciliation. Contact Gretchen directly if you need more hands-on support.

No. Groundwork Foundations is built for any regional or remote employer — agricultural, pastoral, construction, trade, health, retail, or professional services. If you're running a business outside a capital city, this is built for you.
